Activities

Nestled against the backdrop of pristine coastal beauty, our hotel offers a plethora of exhilarating activities that cater to adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Take to the skies and experience the breathtaking views with our paragliding excursions, where trained professionals guide you through the azure sky, providing a bird's eye perspective of the picturesque surroundings.

Scuba Diving

Dive into a world of wonder and discovery with our scuba diving offerings at the hotel, where the magic of the ocean unfolds beneath the surface. Our coastal oasis provides an unparalleled opportunity for enthusiasts and novices alike to explore the mysteries of the underwater realm. Certified instructors are at your service to guide you through the basics or accompany seasoned divers on thrilling underwater expeditions.

Per Person.

Sailing

Embark on a nautical adventure like no other at our hotel, where sailing enthusiasts can indulge in the thrill of navigating the open sea. Our waterfront paradise provides the ideal setting for sailing excursions, offering a fleet of well-maintained vessels and experienced captains ready to guide you through the azure waters.

Per Person.

Surfing

Embrace the rhythm of the ocean at our hotel, where the exhilarating sport of surfing awaits enthusiasts of all levels. Our prime coastal location offers the perfect playground for surfers, with consistent waves that cater to both beginners and seasoned riders. Whether you're catching your first break or perfecting your technique, our expert instructors are on hand to provide personalized guidance.

20$
Per Person.
