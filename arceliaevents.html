MEETINGS & EVENTS

A successful event requires a suitably impressive venue, and Arcelia is the clear choice. The grandeur of our interiors, the splendour of our furnishings, the sheer sense of history and the warmth of our welcome is nothing short of remarkable.

Begin your event planning journey today by making an enquiry with our talented events team.We work closely with clients to understand their event objectives, budget, and preferences.

WEDDINGS

For the perfect wedding day that you will remember for years to come, it's hard to imagine a more magnificent venue. Having welcomed royalty, heads of state and celebrities, our experienced events team are equipped with all the skills, talent and enthusiasm required to make every moment of your special day magical.

THE ARDILAUN ROOM

Arcelia offers flair and attention to detail ensuring that conferences and incentive programmes are unsurpassed occasions. The Ardilaun Suite is perfect for larger events with up to 88 people, providing flexibility for meeting and dining options.It is equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual technology, including projectors, screens, sound systems, and high-speed internet access.

THE CONNAUGHT ROOM

The elegant Connaught Room is available for smaller corporate events, and offers breathtaking views over Lough Corrib and the gardens.

THE BOARDROOM

The Boardroom is perfect for small-sized events, ideal for focused group meetings with natural daylight and fully equipped for your business needs.
