Spa

Welcome to our hotel's luxurious spa, where relaxation and rejuvenation await, where every detail is designed to enhance your well-being. At our hotel's spa, you can escape the stresses of everyday life. Step into a realm of pure bliss, personalized treatments and luxurious amenities await.

ACTIVITY

Arcelia is a favourite destination for those who seek a carefree break with the children. There’s so much to see and do-from falconry to horseback riding – making Arcelia perfect for families.

Gym

Our hotel boasts a modern and well-equipped gymnasium, featuring a range of cardio machines, strength training equipment, and free weights.One can enjoy the convenience of a fully equipped gym right on the premises, eliminating the need to find an external fitness center.

Event Management

We work closely with clients to understand their event objectives, budget, and preferences, coordinate all logistical aspects, including room setup,catering and decorations for Birthday Parties and Weddings.

Breakfast Buffet

Our hotel offers a breakfast buffet with an extensive selection of dishes to cater to various tastes and dietary preferences. From freshly baked pastries to hot and cold breakfast items, there is something for everyone.

Swimming

Our hotel has a well-designed pool beyond just water with awesome lightning and expensive décor,Satisfying of your fitness goals along with a Stress-relieving atmosphere

Theatre

Our hotel offers guests the convenience of enjoying live entertainment or movies without having to leave the hotel premises, featuring state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, and comfortable seating arrangements.

Lobby

The lobby houses the hotel's front desk, where guests check-in and check-out,staffed by hotel personnel. It provides comfortable seating arrangements for guests to relax, wait for their rooms, or meet with others.

Valet Parking

We ensure the safety and security of guests' vehicles. Trained valet attendants handle the parking process carefully, minimizing the risk of accidents or damage. We have designated secured garage for vehicles.
