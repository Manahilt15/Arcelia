FAQS

There is no dress code.

Yes, we welcome children. Please let us know their age and whether a high chair will be required.

Enjoy lighter options and decadent offerings from our breakfast buffet, including our signature slow-baked and glazed whole gammon carved from the trolley, local Irish cold meats and cheeses, smoked salmon, homemade bircher muesli and freshly baked breads and pastries.

Breakfast is served between 8am to 10.45am (November to May) and 7.30am to 10.45am (June to October).
