MAGIC & MAJESTY

Set down your suitcases in the splendid décor of the Arcelia Hotel's lobby. Be overcome with a sensation of well-being. The Indian Sea embraces the terraces of the rooms. Share delightful family moments and let the children experience their own adventures.
